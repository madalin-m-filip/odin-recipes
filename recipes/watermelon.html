Watermelon Agua Fresca

Description

This is a drink served throughout Mexico. It is made with several different fruits. This is a watermelon drink.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • ½ cup water
  • ½ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 4 slices lime
  • 24 fresh mint leaves
  • ice

Steps

  1. Puree the watermelon and water in a blender until smooth. Add sugar to taste. Cut the lime slices in half. Place a half lime slice into each of 8 glasses along with 3 mint leaves. Crush with a cocktail muddler, then fill each glass with ice. Pour in the watermelon agua fresca, and stir before serving.
